Implementation Best Practices

Start with Templates, Then Customize

Begin with proven templates but always customize for your specific prospect and situation. The best results come from combining AI efficiency with human insight.

A/B Testing

Use AI tools to generate multiple email variations and test them against each other. Track metrics like open rates, response rates, and conversion rates to optimize your approach.

Integration Strategy

Plan how your chosen AI tool will integrate with your existing sales stack. Consider data flow between your CRM, email platform, and AI writing tool.
