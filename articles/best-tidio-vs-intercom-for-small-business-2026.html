Making the Right Choice for Your Business

Choose Tidio if you:

  • Run an e-commerce store (especially Shopify or WooCommerce)
  • Need live chat up and running quickly
  • Have a limited budget (under $100/month)
  • Want simple, effective chatbots without complexity
  • Prefer visual, drag-and-drop interfaces

Choose Intercom if you:

  • Need advanced customer segmentation and targeting
  • Require sophisticated automation workflows
  • Have a larger support team (10+ agents)
  • Need comprehensive analytics and reporting
  • Can invest time in setup and training

Bottom Line: Our Recommendation

For 80% of small businesses, Tidio is the clear winner. It delivers excellent live chat functionality, effective chatbots, and e-commerce integrations at a fraction of Intercom's cost. The free plan alone provides more value than many paid competitors.

Intercom makes sense only if you're a rapidly growing company (50+ employees) with complex customer support needs and the budget to match. Its advanced features are impressive but overkill for most small business scenarios.

Start with Tidio's free plan to test the waters. If you outgrow its capabilities and need enterprise-grade features, you can always upgrade to Intercom later. Your wallet—and your team's productivity—will thank you for starting simple.
