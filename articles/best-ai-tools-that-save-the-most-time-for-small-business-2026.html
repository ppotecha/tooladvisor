Common Pitfalls to Avoid

**Over-automation:** Don't automate processes you don't understand. Start simple and build complexity gradually. **Tool overload:** Implement one tool at a time. Too many changes simultaneously can overwhelm your team. **Inadequate training:** AI tools require initial setup and training. Budget time for learning curves. **Ignoring quality:** AI-generated content needs human review. Speed shouldn't compromise quality.
